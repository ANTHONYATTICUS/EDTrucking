Distribution with Performance Team

We delivers flexible end-to-end distribution solutions to meet the needs of our retail, wholesale, and omni-channel customers.

With over 7 million square feet of warehouse space, cutting-edge software platforms, and a hard-working dedicated team, Performance Team is the only partner you need. Our material handling equipment, including pick modules, conveyor and sortation systems, as well as our reach trucks, order pickers, forklifts and pallet jacks ensure that your product gets to market on-time.

Our IT tools include best-in-class Warehouse Management Systems (WMS) from Manhattan & Associates and Softeon. They are robust and flexible providing all the functionality you need to meet your business requirements. The PT Client Zone provides you with 24/7 web portal access to customizable reporting tools and real-time status of your products and their movements. Get all the information you need when you want it, where you want it.
