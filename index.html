Mission & Vision

Logistics from Ed's Transportation

Ed's Transportation is one of the industry-leading logistics companies. Performance Team operates third-party distribution and logistics for companies out of 12 domestic hub cities. Our nearly 30 years of experience offering distribution to companies as well as warehousing, transport and logistics for companies have made Performance Team a global logistics company with an exceptional reputation across retail and manufacturing industries. Strategic location near major ports emphasize Performance Team’s capacities as a port logistics company. Proximity to interstate highways and intermodal rail lines allow ease of transportation distribution as a company. As a national transportation company, location near highways allows us to provide the retail and manufacturing industries with flexible B2B and B2C solutions to offer exceptional product distribution to companies.

Ed's Transportation offers a full suite of retail, wholesale, E-commerce, and omnichannel product distribution for companies. These services include:

  • Pick and Pack
  • Replenishment
  • Garment on Hanger (GOH)
  • Dedicated Facility Management/Contract Logistics
  • Value Added Services
  • Retail Compliance
  • EDI/Interfacing
  • Custom Supply Chain Management
  • Vendor Pick-Up and Consolidation
  • Transloading/Deconsolidation
  • Drayage
  • Pool Point Distribution
  • Local transportation
  • Dedicated Fleet
  • Regional Line Haul
  • Retail Store Delivery
